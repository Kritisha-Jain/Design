My Impact

I’ve organized nationwide protests, public art actions, and educational workshops focused on reclaiming public spaces, creating safety for marginalized genders, and driving policy reform. My work has been featured in British Vogue and multiple conferences across India.

#IWillGoOut — A national protest reclaiming public spaces for women after mass molestation on NYE in Bangalore. I led the Hyderabad chapter and coordinated with national organizers across 30+ cities.

#ChalkTheWalk — A city-wide chalk art campaign visualizing stories of street harassment and protest slogans across public walkways.
