About

I’m Kritisha Jain — an internationally trained, award-winning UX Researcher and Design Strategist with 8+ years of experience across UX research, product design, and creative strategy. I bring human-centered innovation to life through immersive storytelling, participatory methods, and design strategy. I’ve worked with organizations like MathWorks, MIT, Mastercard Foundation, and Amnesty International to shape experiences in engineering tools, social impact technologies, and AI-powered platforms. I believe deeply in inclusive design, and my work has ranged from improving workflows for developers to making makerspaces accessible to the blind.
